Projects

Our devices create cold atmospheric plasma from air. We can do two things with this plasma, on the one hand we can move the air, creating an ionic wind, that we direct in the direction and with the geometry that we want, in a very efficient way. On the other hand, we can use this plasma to innactivate virus, bacteria molds, spores, and even biofilms.

Aeronautics

Being able of direct this wind allows us to design systems with half weight and volume than fan assisted heat sinks, exchanging twice as heat. With the same consumption. We have applications for onboard electronics in drones, round or squared and in a bigger application in in aeronautics. The second picture shows the active heat sink. Also devices that can move a huge amount of air, completely silent and with any geometry.

Drones

In this application we are refrigerating the motor of a drone. With a device that weights less than 10 gr, and consumes 0,8W cools down the motor in the flight.

Biotech

Our technology is based on a physicochemical phenomenon called Corona Discharge It happens when there is a high potential difference between two electrodes, reaching the dielectric strength of air, creating plasma. The plasma is a mist of electrons and ions. The ions travel towards the Collector and on their way collide with the air particles, creating ionic wind. Some of the chemical species generated in this process are ozone particles, nitrogen oxides and hydrogen peroxide. The synergies between them is what makes this technology so powerful.
